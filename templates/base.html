Hey! Meet Sam, our chatbot, who will help you find professors in the MCA block.

Working

  • The Machine Learning-powered chatbot works on an intents-based model.
  • The ML models were created with the help of NTLK and PyTorch 2.0.
  • The Python framework, Flask, serves as the backend.

Features

  • Sam allows you to converse with him via text or audio.
  • He also responed both, via text and audio.
  • Right now Sam speaks only English, but we plan on adding some Indian langauges too.

Talk to Sam

  • Conversing with Sam is very easy and intuitive.
  • To start off, press the little chat icon in the corner.
  • Then, type your question, and press Enter.
  • Or press the microphone option and directly speak to Sam.
